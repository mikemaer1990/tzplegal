What We Do

How We Can Help

Comprehensive legal representation across Ontario. Paralegal expertise — accessible, affordable, and on your side.

Personal Injury

Representation for slip & fall, motor vehicle accidents, and accident benefits before FSRA and LAT.

Traffic Tickets

Fight tickets to protect your driving record, reduce demerit points, and prevent insurance rate increases.

Small Claims Court

Disputes up to $35,000 — unpaid debts, property damage, and contract disagreements handled professionally.

Landlord & Tenant

LTB representation for landlords and tenants — evictions, rent arrears, and maintenance disputes.

Criminal Summary Offences

Skilled representation for summary conviction offences within a paralegal's scope under the LSO.

Notary & Commissioner

Document certification, oaths, and witnessed signatures. Fast, affordable, and professionally handled.
