If you've been injured in an accident, you deserve fair compensation. We represent clients in personal injury matters including slip and fall, motor vehicle accidents, and accident benefits claims before the FSRA and LAT. We fight to ensure your rights are protected throughout the claims process.

A traffic ticket can mean demerit points, higher insurance rates, and even licence suspension. We represent clients at traffic court across Ontario, working to reduce or dismiss charges and protect your driving record. Don't just pay the fine — fight it.

Small Claims Court

Small Claims Court handles disputes up to $50,000 in Ontario. Whether you're pursuing an unpaid debt, property damage, or a contract dispute, we represent both plaintiffs and defendants. We prepare your case, handle the paperwork, and advocate for you in court.

Licensed paralegals in Ontario can represent clients in Small Claims Court, the Ontario Court of Justice (for provincial offences and summary conviction criminal matters), administrative tribunals (including the Landlord and Tenant Board, Human Rights Tribunal, and WSIB), and before various regulatory bodies. They are regulated by the Law Society of Ontario.

Paralegals typically charge significantly less than lawyers — often 50–70% less — because they operate within a defined scope of practice and have lower overhead.
