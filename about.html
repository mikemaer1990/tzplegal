Meet the People Behind the Practice

Paralegal

Johnson Luyiga

Johnson Luyiga specializes in Small Claims Court matters, employment, human rights disputes, Landlord and Tenant Board disputes, Criminal Code Summary Conviction Offences (including firearm prohibition hearings, peace bond hearings, dangerous operation, failure to stop after accident), Provincial Offences, traffic tickets, compliance, Immigration and Refugee Board proceedings, WSIB/WSIAT, and Simple Possession under the Cannabis Act.

His approach emphasizes practical, cost-effective strategies that foster healthy relations and effectively manage conflict. Johnson is fluent in English and three other languages and brings a unique background spanning sport, business, and legal services.

His career began as a football athlete, winning a silver medal at the U17 level. After retiring from competitive sport, he was elected President and General Manager before pivoting to legal services. Johnson is a member in good standing with the Law Society of Ontario and is a sought-after speaker on Equity, Diversity and Inclusion, team dynamics, and workplace culture.
