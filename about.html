Our Story

Built on a Belief That Justice Should Be Accessible

The Zebras Paralegal LP was founded with one mission: to make quality legal representation accessible to everyone in Ontario — not just those who can afford a lawyer.

As a licensed paralegal regulated by the Law Society of Ontario, we bring professional expertise, personal attention, and genuine care to every case we take on. Whether you're facing a traffic ticket or a complex tribunal hearing, you deserve an advocate who takes your matter seriously.

We serve clients across Toronto and London, Ontario, and we're proud to be a trusted legal ally for our community.
